üÜò Troubleshooting Prompts

When Output is Too Generic

This is too generic. Be more specific to [your context]. Use concrete examples and data points.

When You Want Multiple Options

Give me 3 different versions of this: - Version 1: Short and punchy - Version 2: Detailed and thorough - Version 3: Data-driven with numbers

When You Need Sources

Provide sources for each claim. If you're not certain about something, say "I'm not certain, but..." and explain your reasoning.
